Introduction

UI/UX focuses on designing digital experiences that are both visually appealing and easy to use. In this major, you learn to create intuitive interfaces and seamless interactions that improve user satisfaction. It blends creativity with psychology and technology, preparing you to design products that people love to use.

Careers

UX Designer – UX designers research user needs and create intuitive digital experiences. They focus on making websites and apps easy to navigate and enjoyable to use.

UI Designer – UI designers design the visual elements of apps and websites, such as buttons, colors, and typography. Their goal is to create appealing and user-friendly interfaces.

Usability Analyst – These professionals test products to identify usability issues and improve accessibility. They analyze user feedback and suggest design enhancements to optimize user experience.
