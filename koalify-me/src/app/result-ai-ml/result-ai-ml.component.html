Introduction

AI/Machine Learning explores the development of intelligent systems that learn from data and make decisions. This major delves into algorithms, neural networks, and robotics to create applications that mimic human cognition. It’s an exciting field for those fascinated by cutting-edge technology and the potential of machines to transform industries.

Careers

AI Researcher – AI researchers develop new artificial intelligence models and algorithms. Their work advances areas like deep learning, robotics, and natural language processing.

Computer Vision Engineer – These engineers create AI that can interpret images and videos, used in applications like facial recognition, self-driving cars, and medical imaging.

Natural Language Processing (NLP) Engineer – NLP engineers develop AI that understands and processes human language, enabling technologies like chatbots, voice assistants, and automated translations.
