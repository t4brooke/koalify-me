Introduction

Data Science is about extracting meaningful insights from large amounts of data using statistics, programming, and machine learning. This major teaches you how to analyze, visualize, and interpret data to drive informed decisions. It’s a great fit for those who enjoy solving problems with numbers and uncovering patterns that influence real-world outcomes.

Careers

Data Analyst – Data analysts interpret and visualize data to help businesses make informed decisions. They use tools like Python, SQL, and Excel to analyze trends and patterns.

Machine Learning Engineer – These engineers develop AI models that learn from data and make predictions. Their work powers recommendation systems, self-driving cars, and voice assistants.

Data Engineer – Data engineers build and maintain the infrastructure that collects and processes large datasets. They ensure data is structured, clean, and available for analysis.
