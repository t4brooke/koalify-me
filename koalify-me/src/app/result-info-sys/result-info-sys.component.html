Introduction

Information Systems examines how technology and organizations work together to manage and process information. This major bridges the gap between business and technology, teaching you to analyze, design, and implement systems that help organizations run more efficiently. It’s ideal for students who enjoy solving business challenges with innovative tech solutions.

Careers

IT Project Manager – IT project managers oversee technology projects, ensuring they are completed on time and within budget. They coordinate teams, manage risks, and communicate with stakeholders.

Database Administrator – Database administrators manage and optimize databases to store and retrieve information efficiently. They ensure data security, backup processes, and system performance.

Business Intelligence Analyst – These professionals analyze data and generate insights to help businesses make informed decisions. They work with data visualization tools to present complex information in an understandable way.
