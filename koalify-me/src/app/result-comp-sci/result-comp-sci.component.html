Introduction

Computer Science is the study of computing, algorithms, and the theory behind both software and hardware systems. This major blends problem-solving, programming, and mathematics to explore how computers work and how they can solve real-world challenges. It lays the foundation for a wide range of tech careers, from developing innovative software to advancing research in emerging technologies.

Careers

Software Developer – Software developers create applications, websites, and systems that people use daily. They write code, test software, and collaborate with teams to build new digital solutions.

Systems Analyst – Systems analysts evaluate and improve technology systems for businesses, ensuring they run smoothly and efficiently. They often act as a bridge between business needs and technical solutions.

Research Scientist – Computer science researchers work on groundbreaking innovations in areas like artificial intelligence, cryptography, and quantum computing. Their work advances technology and shapes the future of computing.
