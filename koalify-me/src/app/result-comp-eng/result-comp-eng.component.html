Introduction

Computer Engineering is the blend of electrical engineering and computer science, focusing on the design and development of computer hardware and software. This major covers everything from circuit design and microprocessors to embedded systems and software integration. It prepares you to innovate at the intersection of physical computing devices and digital technology.

Careers

Hardware Engineer – Hardware engineers design and develop physical computing components like processors, circuit boards, and memory devices. They ensure that hardware is optimized for performance and efficiency.

Embedded Systems Developer – These developers write software for specialized hardware, such as smartwatches, automotive control systems, and IoT devices. Their work bridges the gap between software and hardware.

FPGA Engineer – FPGA (Field-Programmable Gate Array) engineers work with reconfigurable hardware to create high-performance computing solutions. They are essential in industries like aerospace and telecommunications.
