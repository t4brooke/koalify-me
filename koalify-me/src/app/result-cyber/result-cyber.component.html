Introduction

Cybersecurity is dedicated to protecting computer systems, networks, and data from digital threats. This major covers areas such as encryption, ethical hacking, and risk management, teaching you how to defend against cyber attacks and secure digital environments. It’s a crucial field for those passionate about keeping our increasingly connected world safe.

Careers

Penetration Tester (Ethical Hacker) – Ethical hackers simulate cyberattacks to identify security vulnerabilities before real hackers exploit them. They help organizations strengthen their defenses.

Security Analyst – Security analysts monitor networks and systems for suspicious activity, protecting data from cyber threats. They also develop security policies and respond to incidents.

Incident Responder – When a cyberattack occurs, incident responders investigate the breach, contain the damage, and develop strategies to prevent future attacks. Their role is crucial in mitigating security risks.
