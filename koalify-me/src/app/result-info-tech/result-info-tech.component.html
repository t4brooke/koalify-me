Introduction

Information Technology centers on the practical application and management of computer systems in organizations. This major covers topics such as network management, system administration, and technical support, equipping you with the skills to install, maintain, and improve IT infrastructures in businesses and institutions.

Careers

IT Support Specialist – IT support specialists assist users with technical issues, from software troubleshooting to hardware repairs. They play a vital role in keeping organizations’ tech running smoothly.

Network Administrator – These professionals manage and secure an organization’s computer networks, ensuring stable internet connections and protecting against cyber threats.

Cloud Engineer – Cloud engineers work with platforms like AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure to develop scalable cloud-based solutions. They help businesses store and process data efficiently.
